ROSVENIL HOTEL


P Burgos St, Downtown, Tacloban City, 6500 Leyte

(053) 321 2676


Located in Tacloban, 8.5 km from MacArthur Landing Memorial National Park, Rosvenil Hotel has accommodations with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a patio and a private bathroom with a shower. The rooms have a closet.An àla carte breakfast is available each morning at Rosvenil Hotel. The nearest airport is Daniel Z. Romualdez Airport, 9 km from the accommodation. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip.
