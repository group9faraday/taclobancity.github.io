BINAGOL



It is a delicious treat with a distinctive flavor and distinctive packaging; it is stacked on top of a split coconut shell and wrapped in banana leaves. The name binagol was derived from the word bagol which means coconut shell. Binagol is made from the root crop Taro or Gabi. It is made of mixtures of flour, sugar, milk, and a crushed root crop, yam, gabi.
