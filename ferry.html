BY FERRY



  • The primary ferry port in the area, Ormoc, offers frequent service to Cebu City. On Bohol, there are also some boats that travel from Maasin to Ubay.

  • 3 ports: Matalom to Canigao Island, Port of Inopacan to Cuatro Islas, and Bato ferry port with frequent boats to Cebu City.

  • The RORO ferry connects Surigao to Leyte Island. You must take a 1-hour ferry between Matnog in Sorsogon and Allen in Samar if you are traveling from Luzon by car or boat.
