LAKE DANAO

  • In Ormoc, it is a national park. A pleasant journey with cool mountain air and refreshing drinks to beat the heat. Visitors can kayak, swim, or fish. Or plan to camp; you may also rent cottages on rafts. Best to travel by vehicle or motorcycle.

  • On the Philippine island of Leyte, there is a lake called Lake Danao that resembles a guitar. The lake has a 148 hectare surface area (370 acres). It is located in the Amandiwin mountain range, which is part of the 2,193 hectares (5,420 acres) Lake Danao Natural Park.

  • The lake serves as a source of irrigation for ricelands in various municipalities, including Dagami, Burauen, Pastrana, and at least seven more towns in Eastern Leyte, including Tacloban.
