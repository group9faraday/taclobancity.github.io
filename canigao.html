CANIGAO ISLAND

  • An island located in Matalom, Leyte. This island is famous for its beautiful coral reefs that are good for diving as well as fishing grounds.

  • Canigao Island is also one of the more developed islands in the region, and it has a number of facilities put up for visitors. There are grilling spaces, a treehouse, a few cabins, and an information center. In addition to that, both locals and visitors would like the island's white sand beach, which is surrounded by beautiful palm trees and turquoise waters. A modern paradise, indeed!
