CHOCOLATE MORON



Moron, also known as "malagkit na bigas" in the Philippines, is a rice cake that originated in Tacloban City. It is soft and sticky, smooth and a little oily, and made of glutinous rice. A rice cake called moron, pronounced like "Morong" with the emphasis on the final syllable, is quite similar to suman. It still tastes fantastic whether it is combined with cocoa or chocolate or eaten on its own.
