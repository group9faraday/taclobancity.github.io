MCARTHUR LANDING
MEMORIAL NATIONAL
PARK


  • Located in the municipality of Palo, this is where General McArthur proved his promise, I shall return when they landed to defend the Philippines from the Japanese during the Pacific War. It is one of the main tourist attractions in the area. On July 12, 1977, President Ferdinand Marcos issued Letter of Instructions No. 572 designating the area as a national park.
