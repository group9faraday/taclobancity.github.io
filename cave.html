HINDANG CAVE AND
WILD MONKEYS

  • The province of southern Leyte is where Hindang is situated. The caves of Hindang include wild monkeys. The cave is also conveniently located for both tourists and residents.

  • Hindang monkey caverns are one of the most well-liked day outings in Leyte. In the caves, which are tucked away beneath the mountain's peaks, you can find wild monkeys and a 640-meter zipline is accessible from the summit.
